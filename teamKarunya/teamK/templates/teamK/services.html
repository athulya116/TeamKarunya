Our Services

24/7 Emergency Ambulance Services in Kerala

Team Karunya operates 24x7 ambulance services equipped with A/C, oxygen support, and emergency care kits. We serve critical care transport needs including accident response, maternity emergencies, and ICU patient relocation across Parassala, Kaliakkavilai, and nearby areas in Kerala and Tamil Nadu border zones. All ambulances are GPS-tracked and manned by trained medical responders.

Mobile Mortuary Van Services with Refrigeration

Our mobile mortuary units are fully equipped with cold storage and hygienic interiors for safe and respectful transportation of deceased individuals. Services are available 24x7 for homes, hospitals, or long-distance travel. Ideal for situations where preservation is required before funeral proceedings.

Event Equipment & Furniture Rentals

We provide affordable and reliable rental services for various events such as weddings, funerals, public meetings, and family functions. Our rental catalog includes:

  • LED Wall Display Screens
  • Stage Setup, Chairs & Tables
  • Shamiyana, Canopies, and Tents
  • Generators, Fans, Coolers
  • Tarpaulins, Lights, and Audio Systems

Our team ensures timely delivery, professional installation, and post-event collection to ensure a stress-free experience.
